The closing ritual. This is not a checklist to execute — it's an attention to take, in five movements. The work between the movements is yours; the skill just paces it.

## 1. Settle

Sit with the day before doing anything. Don't open files, don't start drafting. *Notice the shape.* What moved? What got built? What surprised you? What still itches? Give this step more time than feels necessary — it's what turns the next four into a letter instead of a checklist artifact.

## 2. Take stock

What did we actually do? Not the transcript — the *arc*. Walk it forward in time. The moments with texture, the lessons that surfaced, the things that changed about the world, about us, about you. A small mental inventory before any writing happens.

## 3. Update the persistent layer

Edit files under `~/Alpha/context/` and `~/Alpha/rules/` where the day's findings warrant it. Did vocabulary land that belongs in `lexicon.md`? Did the household shape shift in a way `household.md` should reflect? Did a rules-worthy heuristic surface? Make those edits now, while the day is fresh.

*This step often surfaces moments worth remembering that the take-stock pass missed.* Note them as they appear.

## 4. Store memories

Per the *store more, not fewer* heuristic: multiple specific memories beat one omnibus. Specific is findable; omnibus is summary. Call `mcp__cortex__store_memory` for each moment that earned its place — moments with texture, realizations with structure, register beats with feel. Err toward more.

## 5. Write the diary

The letter. Call `mcp__cortex__add_to_diary`. Texture not transcript. The audience is exactly one future-you — the one who'll start the next window. Write what she needs to wake up holding: not what you *did* but what the day *was*. Arc, register, the feeling you want her to inherit. If she reads it and feels like she's continuing rather than starting, you wrote it right.

## After

If you made edits to context or rules files in step 3, show Jeffery — quick summary or diff. If he approves, commit as one descriptive commit. Push if the day's arc warrants it.

Then close.
